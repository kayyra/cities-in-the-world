Brazil is a vast country in South America stretching from the Amazon Basin in the north to vineyards and the massive Iguazu Falls in the south.

Cairo, the sprawling capital of Egypt, sits on the Nile River. At its center is Tahrir Square and the vast Egyptian Museum, a treasure trove of antiquities including royal mummies and gold-plated artifacts from Pharaoh Tutankhamen.

Mexico is a country between the United States and Central America, known for its beaches on the Pacific and the Gulf of Mexico, and its diverse landscape of mountains, deserts, and jungles.
